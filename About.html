About Me

Darrian is a talented and passionate artist, designer, and writer who enjoys all aspects of the creative process. She has published over 60 cover designs that have been widely enjoyed by customers in the United States, United Kingdom, and Canada, which led her to launch her second e-commerce business: Designs By Darrian.

She has had the pleasure of designing posters and spearheading event branding for both the Strome and Forestburg Curling Clubs. As well as logo design for Lake Country Group, and @apartmentdrummer, the latter of which allowed her to employ her skills in web design as well.

Darrian is also an award winning photographer with photos featured by Tourism Calgary, and has done employee headshots for both Long and McQuade Musical Instruments and Chinook School of Music.

Outside of art and design Darrian is a published writer with several articles and guides featured by TheGamer and she hopes to one day become a published poet and novelist.
